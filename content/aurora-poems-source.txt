AURORA TO STARRY NIGHTS
*Poetry Collection*
*Sanyogita Shukla*

*This book is dedicated to all those who have ever felt deeply, whose hearts have navigated the spaces between the first light of dawn and the quiet, starry depths of the night.

*Keeping this journey with ... because it's never ending and there is much more to my journey*

Written To be Felt
------------------

Some unsaid is just said,
Which was supposed to be expressed

Some tears fell,
Which were supposed to be held

Some heartbeats were left unheard,
Which were supposed to be heard

Some hands were held back,
Which were supposed to be felt

Some lies were said,
Which were supposed to be unsaid

Some unsaid was made to be seen,
Which was supposed to be underneath

Truths were ruthless
Hands were fisted
Lips twisted
Words deeply hurt
Expressions remain unchanged

Some turned back
Some agitated
Ego didn't bend
Some folded hands

This is how two people were made to separate,
Two different roads which went in parallel but never met...

Whether it's a day or night

Selfish lover
-------------

A poem which made me realize that I am a selfish lover

I told you that
You were my home
And wherever you aspire to go
You will belong to me
Wherever you go
You will come back to me

But when you left
I realized that you were shelter to me
I just don't want you to belong to me
But also expects to belong to you

ISN'T IT CHAOTIC TO THINK?
----------------------------

I am trying to get something
Maybe that I can never own
Am I walking towards something
I should be running away from

Trying to get validated for everything
That I was not even thinking to become

Just for the sake of standing out in their eyes
I know the thought was not wise

Sometimes expecting them to own me
Sometimes expecting them to choose me
Sometimes expecting them to stand for me
Sometimes expected them to hold on to me

And I turned into someone
That they never want
And I became a chaos
Maybe that I never wanted to be

I was with them,
with their faults,
with their likes,
with their dislikes,
with their wants,
with their don'ts

And they never get to see the real me
Because all I did was just to stand in their eyes
I know the thought was not wise

Because they were never satisfied with that version of me
And I was never that person that I tried to be

In all I am at the verge of dislikes and hates
I don't understand why I can't be the real me
Maybe I am just too scared to lose them

But is it fine to hold on those that expect you to be someone

Maybe the one that you never wanted to be

But then again another thought comes in the game
What if you earn them, with this change in yourself

But can I ever be happy to be with them on cost of losing me

Or will I win all my life by earning them
Because that's all I ever wanted in my life

And with these thoughts
I walked towards something
That I should be running away from...

To love selflessly

Choosing Myself

No more lies, No more issues
Letting myself something else to choose

In the wake of society's affirmation
Amidst lies, chaos and tension

Making everyone happy and satisfied
Unsatisfied mind and hopeless heart gets into a fight

Lose your needs in their desire
Society has a big hold
Till now this is what I was told
Has burdened me higher and higher

To stand in their eyes
I tried and tried
I know the thought was not wise
Has taken my smile on stake

Not letting others decide what I want
Will lose my identity this is what haunts

Roads are diverged, diverged for me
Taking the one which will meet from me to me.
